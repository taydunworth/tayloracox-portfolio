CMS Summit 2018

To expand my skill set in the tech industry, I spent the past year or so transitioning from the title of graphic designer to front-end developer and confronting the age-old debate of "should designers learn to code?"

Sure, the two roles are closely related, but how do you make that pivot in a short amount of time? Do you completely abandon your past life as a designer and fully embrace your newfound love of development or embrace their coexistence?

This talk discusses my journey, how I made the pivot between the two careers, and the positives and negatives of having a background in design as a developer.
